On Nov. 16, I participated in 2022 AMC 10 B at Oyster River High School, here's my feeling and some solutions of the contest.

Problem 6

My performance is well until I met problem 6. "How many of the first ten numbers of the sequence \(121, 11211, 1112111, \ldots\) are prime numbers?" I was about to try them one by one but I thought if I continue trying, it won't have enough time for the remaining problem, so I have to give up. Until I found the solution that is \[ \forall n\in\mathbb{Z}^+,\ \frac{10^{2n+2}-1}{9}+10^{n}=\frac{10^{n+1}-1}{9}(10^n+1) \]
